About TEDx SCU

At universities, we come from a multitude of exciting backgrounds, with a variety of unique ideas to share. TED Talks provide the opportunity for us to communicate our knowledge of the world through amazing presentations and inspiring stories. TEDx, or independently organized TED events, was started with the mission of "ideas worth spreading." All events are fully planned and coordinated on a community-by-community basis.


This year we are bringing TED talks to SCU. TEDxSantaClaraUniversity will be a professional-grade production occurring on Tuesday May 10th, 2016 at Santa Clara’s Music Recital Hall. It will feature a mix of student and non-student speakers, with an array of topics that center upon this year’s theme: Paradigm Shifts: Thinking Outside the Box. Our speakers will give talks set to challenge the status quo and inspire unconventional thinking.

About TED

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 25 years ago, TED has grown to support those world-changing ideas with multiple initiatives. The annual TED Conference invites the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes. Their talks are then made available, free, at TED.com.
